Favorite Lesson:

My favorite lesson during the Introduction to Computing subject is HTML/CSS. Even though it's so challenging because of the many syntaxes and key sensitivity, I find it fun because it has plenty to offer and do, and there's also a good feeling when you get the logic or know how to create a website.

Lessons:

During the Introduction to Computing subject, I realized computing encompasses more than just basic calculations; it involves using technology for data management and problem-solving. It broadened my awareness of various global tech career options. I explored IPOS (Input, Process, Output, Storage) as a foundational tech concept with potential future relevance. Also, I dived into computer security, a field dedicated to protecting data and devices from unauthorized access and potential harm. Furthermore, I got introduced to HTML and CSS, where CSS enhances visuals and HTML structures web content. Mastering these demands logical thinking and memorization, but the sense of accomplishment upon succeeding is highly fulfilling.
